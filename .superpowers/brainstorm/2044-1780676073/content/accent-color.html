Secondary Accent — Sales & Urgency Color

Flash sales, discount badges, countdown timers, and limited stock indicators need a "hot" accent that creates urgency without clashing with indigo.

A

Coral Red (#EF4444)

Classic urgency red. Universally understood as "sale/deal/hot." High contrast against white and indigo. Risk: can feel aggressive if overused.

B

Amber Orange (#F59E0B)

Warm, energetic, deal-focused. Less aggressive than red but still eye-catching. Works great for flash sale banners. Pairs naturally with indigo.

C

Hot Pink (#EC4899)

Modern, playful urgency. Stands out without the cliché red "SALE" vibe. Works if VNShop skews younger. Think Klarna, Afterpay deals badges.
