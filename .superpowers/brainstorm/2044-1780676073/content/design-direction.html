What visual direction should VNShop take?

Pick the mood that best fits your brand — this drives color, typography, spacing, and component style

Minimal Luxury

Minimal Luxury

Dark neutrals + gold accent. Serif headings, generous whitespace, subtle animations. Think Apple Store meets high-end fashion. Premium feel, less visual noise.

Soft Modern

Soft Modern

Indigo primary + soft grays. Rounded corners, subtle gradients, friendly sans-serif. Think Linear/Vercel. Clean, trustworthy, tech-forward but approachable.

Warm Organic

Warm Organic

Burnt orange + warm neutrals. Large rounded corners, earthy tones, hand-crafted feel. Think Etsy meets Airbnb. Inviting, local-market warmth, human touch.

Bold Vibrant

Bold Vibrant

Purple-to-cyan gradients on dark base. Strong typography, glass morphism, neon accents. Think Discord meets gaming marketplaces. Energetic, youthful, distinctive.
