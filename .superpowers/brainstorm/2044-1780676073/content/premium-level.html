How premium should VNShop feel?

Minimal Luxury has a range — from accessible-premium to full haute couture. Where should VNShop land?

A

Accessible Premium

Clean and elevated but still friendly. Like Uniqlo or Muji — quality design that doesn't intimidate. Good spacing, refined type, but not cold. Works for a marketplace with diverse price points.

B

Confident Modern

Polished and assertive. Like Apple Store or Stripe — precise typography, bold contrasts, subtle motion. Communicates trust and quality. Slightly more serious than A.

C

High-End Editorial

Magazine-quality layouts, dramatic whitespace, large imagery. Like Mr Porter or SSENSE. Beautiful but can feel distant. Best if VNShop skews toward premium/curated products.
