Typography — Font Strategy

Marketplace UI needs high readability at small sizes across thousands of product titles, prices, and labels.

A

Inter Only

One font for everything — headings, body, labels, prices. Clean, fast loading, excellent at small sizes. GitHub, Linear, Vercel all use this approach. Simple, consistent, no font conflicts.

B

Inter + Plus Jakarta Sans

Jakarta Sans for headings (slightly rounder, more friendly), Inter for body/UI. Adds personality without sacrificing readability. A subtle upgrade over single-font — Gumroad, Notion approach.

C

Geist (or system stack)

Vercel's Geist font or pure system fonts (-apple-system, Segoe UI). Zero load time, native feel, extremely crisp. Most performant option — matters when pages have 50+ product cards.
